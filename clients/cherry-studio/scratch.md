
**Step-by-Step Guide to Enable "Auto Save" in Cherry Studio:**

1. **Open Cherry Studio**: Launch the application.
2. **Access Preferences**:
   - Click the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner.
   - Select **Preferences** from the dropdown menu.
3. **Enable Autosave**:
   - Navigate to the **General** tab.
   - Check the **"Autosave"** checkbox to prevent data loss.
4. **Customize Backup Location**:
   - In the **Backup Location** section, specify your dedicated folder (e.g., `~/prompthub/clients/cherry-studio/backup`).
5. **Enable Cloud Sync (Optional)**:
   - Go to the **Cloud Sync** tab.
   - Enable syncing and configure Google Drive account details (if not already set up).

**Notes**:
- Confirm these settings in Cherry Studio’s preferences or refer to its documentation for exact locations.
- Ensure your backup folder is accessible and has sufficient storage.
